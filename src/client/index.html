Welcome!

Welcome to TransKnowledge, a web application designed from the ground up to give transgender people the information they need.

Click on any of the buttons above to go to one of the four applications.

Map has a map of the United States, along with the current climate of trans laws in each state (detailed information will be added in the full release). News contains several curated news feeds for trans issues, along with the ability to make your own. Forum has an anonymous forum for asking questions. Resources contains an up-to-date list of trustworthy and moderated resources for trans people, along with the ability to send in your own to be moderated.
